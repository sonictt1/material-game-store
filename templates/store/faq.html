Privacy Policy

When you purchase a key I take your email address for future reference (in case you need me to look up past keys you have purchased). Other than that I save no information. Payment processing is handled by Stripe. This website never actually sees your payment information. Stripe handles all of that and lets me know if the payment went through. I do reserve the right to add analytics to my site in order to decide how to focus my efforts for future development. If this happens I will update this policy accordingly.

Your information is encrypted in my server and my server is protected by secure development practices. Even if an attacker gained access to my database they'd have to decrypt your email address in order to get it, which would take a ludicrous amount of time. Traffic to and from this site is secured with SSL encryption. This prevents an attacker from stealing your information en-route to my server or en-route to Stripe's server.
